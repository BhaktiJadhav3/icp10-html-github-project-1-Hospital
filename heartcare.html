Heart Care :

                  

Everyday is a Fresh Start It's never too late or too early to invest in your heart health. When we take care of Our Heart we reduce our risk of heart disease.

World Heart Day is annually observed on September 29 all over the world. World Heart Federation and the World Health Organisation together came up with the idea of World Heart Day in 2000. The reason was to create a day that would raise awareness and mobilize global action against cardiovascular disease.

How to take care of your heart ?

  • Eat a heart healthy diet
  • If overweight, lose weight
  • Do Some Cardio
  • Live Smoke-free
  • Get Adequate Sleep
  • See a Doctor for Regular Checkups
  • Drink More Water

Some types of heart disease include:

1.Coronary Artery Disease (CAD):
The most common form, caused by the buildup of plaque in the coronary arteries, leading to reduced blood flow to the heart.
2. Heart Attack:
Occurs when blood flow to a part of the heart is blocked, causing damage to the heart muscle.
3. Arrhythmias:
Abnormal heart rhythms that can disrupt normal blood flow, sometimes leading to serious complications.
4. Heart Valve Disease:
Involves damage to or a defect in one of the heart's valves, affecting blood flow.
5. Congenital Heart Defects:
Structural problems with the heart that are present at birth.
