JOINT REPLACEMENT SURGERIES WE PROVIDE

At WELLNESS HOSPITAL, we offer a variety of joint replacement surgeries to meet the unique needs of our patients. Our expert team of surgeons specializes in the following procedures:

1. Knee Replacement

Knee replacement surgery is performed when the knee joint is damaged beyond repair. This procedure involves removing damaged cartilage and bone and replacing it with a prosthetic joint. We offer both partial and total knee replacement options.

2. Hip Replacement

Hip replacement surgery is one of the most common procedures we perform. It involves replacing the worn-out or damaged hip joint with a prosthetic. This surgery can alleviate chronic hip pain and improve joint function dramatically.

3. Shoulder Replacement

Shoulder replacement surgery is performed to replace the shoulder joint with a prosthetic. This is commonly done for patients with severe arthritis or after a major injury to the shoulder joint.

4. Elbow Replacement

Elbow replacement surgery is recommended for patients suffering from conditions like rheumatoid arthritis or after a severe fracture. The procedure involves replacing the damaged parts of the elbow joint with an artificial joint.

5. Ankle Replacement

Ankle replacement surgery is less common but highly effective for patients suffering from severe arthritis in the ankle. This surgery can help restore mobility and reduce chronic pain.

Preparing for Joint Replacement Surgery

Our team will guide you through the entire pre-surgery process to ensure that you are fully prepared for your joint replacement surgery. This includes:

1.Pre-Surgery Consultation

During your consultation, you will meet with one of our orthopedic surgeons to discuss your condition, medical history, and the details of your surgery. Our team will also perform a comprehensive physical exam and diagnostic imaging.

2.Preoperative Tests

We will conduct necessary preoperative tests such as blood work, EKG, and imaging studies to ensure that you are healthy and ready for surgery.

3.Physical Therapy Planning

Before surgery, we will work with you to develop a physical therapy plan that will help you recover faster and regain mobility post-surgery.
